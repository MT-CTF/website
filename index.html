Map Makers Wanted!

I need good maps to add to the game. I'm looking for variety of gameplay, so maps should be sufficiently different to encourage different play styles.

You can use Minetest Game and any mapgen to find locations. Once you find a suitable location, you can modify it to your wishes and then export it using the GUI provided by the ctf_map mod. Alternatively, you can tell me your map ideas or give me good seeds and locations.
